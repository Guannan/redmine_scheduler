redmine_scheduler by milt

A lab coach/tutor scheduling plugin for Redmine.

A lab coach/tutor scheduling plugin for Redmine. In development for in-house use at the Johns Hopkins University Digital Media Center, but free for all to implement! VERY EARLY STAGE!!!

Dependencies

Redmine 1.2.0

Install

(run: export RAILS_ENV="production" beforehand if you don't have that set. 1. unzip to [redmine app dir]/vendors/plugins/redmine_scheduler 2. run: db:migrate_plugins
